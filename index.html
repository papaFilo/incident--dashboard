FINALIZING INVESTIGATION REPORT

Incident Overview

On June 25, 2026 at 11:00 WIB, a generator unit belonging to PT. Unisat at Gemah #4 Pad experienced a thermal incident. The generator was operating as backup for GE-6 ESP system. The fire was successfully extinguished using 4 APAR units with no injuries reported.

Direct Causes

  • Unbalanced fan belt pulley (cracks) → fan belt snapped → engine ran without cooling Mechanical
  • ESD system malfunction prevented automatic engine shutdown during overheating Safety System
  • Overheat damaged cylinder #5 nozzle seal → diesel fuel leak Secondary Damage
  • Fire triangle elements fulfilled (heat + fuel + oxygen) Ignition

Indirect Causes

Personal Related
  • Supervision coverage not extended to all operational hours
  • Operator fatigue due to extended working hours
  • Insufficient refresher training for early warning detection
Job Related
  • No predictive maintenance program for early degradation detection
  • No technical assessment for ESD system functionality
  • Inadequate preventive maintenance implementation

Root Causes

  • No SOP for "Equipment Readiness Inspection" Procedural
  • No predictive maintenance program Maintenance
  • No structured supervision schedule Supervision
  • No periodic ESD technical assessment Technical
